Marketing Numbers

a New Source of Revenue and Value

After implimenting Marketing Numbers, only clients that signed up for the premium plans could have access to the Marketing Numbers feature.

 Problem:

Previous system could only track lead sources from a general marketing campaign not from marketing campaigns that have specific criteria.

  • Our sales reps would ask customers, "How did you hear about us?" which customers would respond, "Google, Bilboard ad, TV commercial, ect.."
  • Our clients wanted to track leads that came from Google PPC, Google SEO, and other specific marketing campaigns which made the current system less valuable.

If a customer calls our client's "Google PPC" phone number, then our Sales Rep's form will automatically populate the lead source question with "Google PPC" before the customer is connected with our Sales Rep.

Solution:

I would provide our clients with phone numbers for their individual marketing campaigns.

  • Using five9's IVA (inteligent voice automation), I developed over 100 custom IVA routes for 8 different clients.
  • Each route would define a lead source variable for every call dialed to that route.
  • The lead source variable would attach to the inbound connector as a parameter for our Sales Rep's scripting form.
  • The scripting form would dynamically fill the lead source question completely based on which phone number the customer called.
